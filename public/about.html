About Quinoa

Quinoa is an ancient grain that is organically grown in mostly in South America with high concentrations of Calcium, Phosphorus, and Iron and is replacing Wheat as the number one grain worldwide. Quinoa also has a higher Protein content than any of the other grains, as well as being Gluten-free.

About J Vodka

J Vodka is the finest tasting Vodka being manufacturd in the wold made form Quinoa. This gives it a uniquely delicious flavor. Quinoa imparts a slightly sweet, nutty aroma and flavor to the spirit, while the Wheat gives it structure and a delayed attack at room temperature. When chilled, the Wheat takes a back seat to the viscous and nutty, almost mineral, quality of the Quinoa.
